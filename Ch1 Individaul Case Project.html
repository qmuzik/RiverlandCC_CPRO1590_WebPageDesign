Meet Danny Muzik

Born and Raised in Austin, Minnesota Danny is well known around his community. His parents are Janice and Conrad Muzik, owners of the old Muzik Shoppe store and Music instructors. Danny is Married to Mary Muzik and has two loving children, Quincy and Ella. They have three dogs Gabby, Barney and Oakley. He started his career path by playing drums with his parent during their Music shows in the Austin school district and working for Nelson Sound owned by the late Joel Nelson. Now for the last twenty years Danny has been doing Drywall and Painting as his main Job. Nelson Sound is Danny's and Kenneth Milands second job, providing sound equipment to places such as fairs, schools, etc. Danny is a hardwarking man who will get your job fast and in the best way possible.
